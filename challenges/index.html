📖 How to Use This Challenge

  1. Read the theory for the day's topic
  2. Solve the problems — try on your own for 20 min before looking at hints
  3. Review the solution approach (collapsible hints below each problem)
  4. Mark the day as complete by clicking the checkbox
  5. Consistency > Perfection — doing 2 problems daily beats 20 in one day
💡 Golden Rule

Spend at most 30-45 minutes on a problem. If stuck, read the approach, understand it, and re-implement from scratch. Understanding > memorizing.
